Frequently Asked Questions

Will my Excel formatting be preserved?

Yes! Our converter preserves all cell formatting, colors, fonts, and formulas (displayed as values). Tables, charts, and conditional formatting are maintained in the PDF.

Can I convert multiple worksheets?

Absolutely! You can choose to convert the first sheet, active sheet, or all worksheets. For multiple sheets, each worksheet becomes a separate page in the PDF.

What's the best page size for printing?

A4 is recommended for international documents. Choose "Letter" for US printing. Use "Fit to page" to automatically adjust content to the selected page size.

How organized will the PDF be?

Very organized! The PDF will include:

  • Exact Excel layout and formatting
  • Professional headers and footers (optional)
  • Page numbers for multi-page documents
  • Print-ready margins and spacing
  • Searchable text (not just images)
