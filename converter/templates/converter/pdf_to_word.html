Frequently Asked Questions

Will my document be well-organized in Word?

Yes! Our converter preserves headings, paragraphs, lists, and tables. Choose "Preserve layout" for exact formatting or "Text only" for clean extraction.

What's the best format for editing?

.docx is recommended for full Microsoft Word compatibility with all formatting features. Use .txt for plain text or .rtf for basic formatting.

Does it work with scanned PDFs?

Yes! Enable "Enhanced OCR" option for scanned documents. This uses optical character recognition to extract text from images.

How organized will the Word document be?

Very organized! The converted document will have:

  • Proper headings and subheadings
  • Organized paragraphs and lists
  • Preserved tables and images
  • Consistent fonts and spacing
  • Page breaks in correct places
