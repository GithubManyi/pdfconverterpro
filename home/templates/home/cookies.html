Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Key Functions of Cookies

  • Enable basic website functionality and security features
  • Remember your preferences and settings for future visits
  • Analyze how visitors use our PDF conversion tools
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Maintain your session during file conversion processes

Types of Cookies Used on PDF Converter Pro

We use different types of cookies to provide and improve our PDF conversion services:

Essential Cookies (Necessary)

These cookies are strictly necessary for our website to function:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your session during file uploads and conversions
  • Security Cookies: Protect against malicious attacks and abuse
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across servers for optimal performance
  • CSRF Protection: Prevent cross-site request forgery attacks

Note: These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for basic functionality.

Analytics & Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our PDF tools:

  • Usage Analytics: Track which conversion tools are most popular
  • Performance Monitoring: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Conversion Success Rates: Monitor file conversion success/failure rates
  • Feature Improvement: Gather data to improve tool functionality

All analytics data is fully anonymized and cannot identify individual users.

Preference & Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences:

  • Theme Preference: Remember dark/light mode selection
  • Language Settings: Store preferred language (if applicable)
  • Recent Tools: Remember frequently used conversion tools
  • UI Preferences: Store interface customization choices

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have full control over cookies. Here's how to manage them:

Cookie Consent Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent tool:

Browser Settings

Control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

What to Consider

  • Disabling cookies may break some website features
  • You'll need to set preferences on each visit
  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled for security
  • You can delete existing cookies at any time

Cookie Duration & Storage

Cookies on PDF Converter Pro have different lifespans based on their purpose:

Cookie Type Duration Purpose
Session Cookies Until browser closes Temporary session management
Preference Cookies Up to 1 year Remember user settings
Analytics Cookies Up to 2 years Usage tracking

Your Consent & Rights

By using PDF Converter Pro, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You have the right to:

  • Withdraw Consent: Disable non-essential cookies at any time
  • Access Information: Request details about cookies used
  • Delete Cookies: Remove existing cookies from your browser
  • File Complaints: Contact data protection authorities if concerned

To withdraw consent for non-essential cookies, use the cookie management tool above or adjust your browser settings.
