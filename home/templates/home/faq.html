Try these steps:

Why is formatting lost in PDF to Word conversion?

Complex layouts with multiple columns, text boxes, or unusual fonts may not convert perfectly. For best results:

  • Use simple, single-column layouts
  • Avoid embedded fonts when possible
  • For scanned documents, try the OCR option
