1. Introduction & Scope

Welcome to PDF Converter Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our file conversion services.

This policy applies to information we collect:

  • On our website (pdfconverterpro.com)
  • In email, text, and other electronic messages between you and PDF Converter Pro
  • When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This policy complies with data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Uploaded Files

When you use our file conversion services, you may upload documents for processing. We handle these files with the utmost care:

  • Temporary Storage: Files are temporarily stored on our secure servers during processing
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded files are automatically deleted within 1 hour of processing completion
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not archive, back up, or permanently store your files
  • No Human Access: Files are processed automatically without human review
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share uploaded files with any third parties

Note: We cannot access the content of your files after you download the converted version.

2.2 Technical & Usage Information

We collect anonymous technical data to improve our services:

Technical Data

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device information
  • Screen resolution

Usage Data

  • Pages visited
  • Conversion types used
  • Session duration
  • Errors encountered
  • Conversion success rates

All technical data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual users from this data.
